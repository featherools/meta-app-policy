Data Deletion Request

Last Updated: February 2025

If you wish to delete your personal data from our system, please follow the steps below:

How to Request Data Deletion

Send an email to your-email@example.com with the subject **"Data Deletion Request"** and include the following details:

  • Your registered email or username
  • Reason for deletion (optional)

Processing Time

Your request will be processed within 7 business days, and you will receive a confirmation email once your data has been removed.

Contact Us

If you have any questions, contact us at your-email@example.com.
